About Sugar - Indian Sugar Exporter

Neelakant Impex supplies and exports Indian Sugar like white refined S30, M30, and ICUMSA grades (45, 100, 600-1200). With sourcing from reputed Indian mills and Brazilian suppliers, we deliver premium, sparkling white sugar suitable for domestic and global consumption. These varieties are refined, free-flowing, and used in various culinary, commercial, and industrial applications. From daily table sugar to export-quality ICUMSA, we ensure purity, moisture control, and consistent grain size. Backed by strong logistics, we provide container shipments and bulk orders with reliable delivery timelines.

Our Sugar Varieties

S30 White Refined Sugar

S30 White Refined Sugar is a premium grade of fine, sparkling white crystals with high purity. It is one of the most refined and widely distributed sugar grades in India and abroad. The fine grain size ensures quick solubility, making it perfect for sweets, syrups, and baking. Free from contaminants and dust, it is processed using modern centrifugal technology. Ideal for FMCG packaging, confectionery, soft drinks, and pharmaceutical uses. Its uniform texture and appealing color make it a staple in domestic and industrial kitchens.

Specifications
  • Crystal Size: 0.8–1.2 mm
  • Moisture: 0.04% Max
  • Polarization: 99.8% Min
  • Ash Content: 0.04%
  • Solubility: 100% Free Flowing
  • Shelf Life: 2–3 years
  • Smell: Odor Free

M30 White Refined Sugar

M30 Sugar is a popular grade of crystal sugar, widely used in households and food industries. It is medium-sized, white in color, and free-flowing, ensuring easy handling and mixing. Known for its high purity and low moisture content, making it ideal for daily consumption. The consistent crystal size enhances dissolvability in beverages like tea and coffee. It meets FSSAI standards and is hygienically processed from the finest sugarcane. M30 sugar is suitable for retail packs, institutional use, and bulk supply worldwide.

Specifications
  • Crystal Size: 1.4–1.7 mm
  • Moisture: 0.04% Max
  • Polarization: 99.8% Min
  • Foreign Matter: <0.20%
  • Free Flowing & Odor Free
  • Purity: 99.9%

ICUMSA 45 White Sugar

ICUMSA 45 is a highly refined white sugar with an exceptional degree of purity. Known for its sparkling white color and fine granules, it's commonly used in food production and direct consumption. It dissolves quickly, making it ideal for beverages and syrups. This sugar is produced under stringent international standards. ICUMSA 45 is popular in the global export market due to its consistency and quality. It is free from harmful substances, making it safe and preferred worldwide.

Specifications
  • Crystal Size: 0.8–1.2 mm
  • Polarization: 99.8% Min
  • Moisture: 0.04% Max
  • Solubility: 100%
  • Purity: 99.9%
  • Origin: Brazil

ICUMSA 100 Sugar

ICUMSA 100 sugar is a white crystalline sugar with slightly higher color units than ICUMSA 45. It maintains good purity and is often used in food processing and household applications. This sugar offers excellent dissolvability and mild sweetness. ICUMSA 100 is refined under hygienic conditions and meets export requirements. It is commonly sourced from sugarcane via refined crystallization. Its moderate color makes it versatile for various industrial and culinary uses.

Specifications
  • Color: Sparkling White
  • ICUMSA: 100 Max
  • Polarization: 99.8% Min
  • Moisture: 0.04% Max
  • Solubility: 100%
  • Smell: Odorless
  • Usage: Industrial & Household

ICUMSA 150 Sugar

ICUMSA 150 is a light brown or golden sugar commonly used in bakeries and food manufacturing. It has more molasses content compared to ICUMSA 45 and 100. Its color and rich flavor make it ideal for sauces, cereals, and confectionery. This grade is partially refined and suitable for consumption after minimal processing. It is in high demand in Africa, Asia, and Latin American countries. ICUMSA 150 offers a balance between purity and flavor richness.

Specifications
  • Color: White
  • ICUMSA: 150 Max
  • Polarization: 99.6% Min
  • Moisture: 0.05% Max
  • Description: Free Flowing, Odor Free
  • Usage: Beverages, Bakery

ICUMSA 600–1200 Brown Sugar

This type of sugar is raw and less refined, typically golden to dark brown in color. It retains natural molasses, offering a rich flavor profile and coarse texture. Raw sugar is mainly used in refineries for further processing. It is produced through the initial crystallization of cane juice. Popular in countries where unrefined sugar is used in traditional cooking. It contains natural nutrients and is often considered more wholesome.

Specifications
  • Color: Brown
  • ICUMSA: 600–1200
  • Polarization: 98.0% Min
  • Moisture: 0.10% Max
  • Crystals: Coarse
  • Use: Industrial, Distillery

Refined Sulphurless Sugar

Refined Sulphurless Sugar is a high-purity white sugar produced without the use of sulfur during clarification. It is processed using modern techniques like phosphoric acid and ion exchange, ensuring food safety. This sugar has a bright white color, fine crystals, and no residual chemicals. It is ideal for health-conscious consumers and industries needing chemical-free ingredients. Often used in confectionery, beverages, pharmaceuticals, and baby food. Its purity, taste, and safety make it a preferred choice in domestic and export markets.

Specifications
  • Color: Crystal White
  • Processing: No Sulphur Used
  • Polarization: 99.9% Min
  • Moisture: 0.02% Max
  • Note: Suitable for Health-Conscious Use
  • Solubility: 100%

Demerara Sugar

Demerara Sugar is a minimally processed, coarse-textured sugar with a rich golden-brown color. It retains natural molasses, giving it a mild caramel flavor and a subtle crunch. Popular for topping baked goods, cereals, and coffee due to its distinctive texture. Produced from pure cane juice through simple crystallization without heavy refining. Its large crystals and unique taste make it a gourmet choice in kitchens worldwide. Demerara sugar is valued for both its flavor and natural appeal in artisanal recipes.

Specifications
  • Color: Golden Brown
  • Crystals: Large, Crunchy
  • Flavor: Rich Molasses
  • Purity: 97%
  • Usage: Coffee, Desserts
  • Origin: Natural Cane

Muscovado Sugar

Muscovado Sugar is an unrefined cane sugar known for its dark brown color and moist texture. It retains high levels of natural molasses, giving it a deep, rich flavor with hints of toffee. Commonly used in baking, sauces, marinades, and gourmet desserts for its bold taste. Unlike refined sugars, it undergoes minimal processing, preserving essential minerals. Its sticky consistency and intense flavor enhance recipes requiring a robust sweetness. Muscovado is a favorite among chefs and health-conscious consumers for its authenticity.

Specifications
  • Color: Dark Brown
  • Texture: Moist and Sticky
  • Molasses Content: Rich
  • Flavor: Deep Caramel
  • Usage: Baking, Sauces
  • Processing: Unrefined and Natural
