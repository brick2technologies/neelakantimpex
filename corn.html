About Corn

Corn is a grain which is consumed all around the world, and is favored by everyone. Though the grain has rich nutritional values but excess of consumption can increase blood pressure. Corn is a difficult crop to be found everywhere. It requires hot climate with good amount of rain for it to grow decorously. In India, corn or maize is considered to be a kharif crop, which means “summer crop”. It is the third most important crop of the nation. Though India is not one of the top contenders in production, but it exports a huge amount of corn throughout the world. We can cater your demand of all the three types of corn. Maize, White corn, Yellow corn. There is not much difference in nutritional value, it’s just the naturally occurring substances which changes the color of corn found in different areas. We also supply GMO / NON - CORN / White CORN for Human and animal Consumption. We supply corn from Brazil, Argentina, Ukraine and USA also.
