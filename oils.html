About Our Edible Oils Exporter India

NEELAKANT IMPEX has over 15 years of expertise in supplying high-quality edible oils to domestic and global markets. Our oils are specifically tailored for restaurants, food manufacturers, retailers, and households. We use the latest refining techniques, ensuring purity, clarity, and extended shelf life with antioxidants. Our wide range includes refined and crude oils such as Sunflower, Soybean, Corn, and Palm. All oils are packaged hygienically in various sizes—375ml bottles to 36,000lt tankers. Committed to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction, we are a trusted name in the edible oil industry.

Edible Oils Varieties

Refined Sunflower Oil

Extracted from premium sunflower seeds, this oil is clear, bright, and light. Rich in polyunsaturates and Vitamin E, it promotes heart health and immunity. Known for its neutral taste, it's ideal for daily frying and cooking. Packed in a hygienic setup with added vitamins A & D for family nutrition. Water-like clarity and strong aroma retention ensure freshness in dishes. The antioxidant blend helps increase shelf-life and stability for long-term use.

Specifications
  • Specific Gravity: 0.916–0.923
  • FFA: Max 0.1%
  • Moisture: Max 0.1%
  • Appearance: Clear and Bright
  • Odor: Neutral
  • Iodine Value: 120–143

Refined Corn Oil

Refined Corn Oil is light yellow with a clear, transparent appearance. It has a neutral taste and odor, making it ideal for salads and deep frying. Packed with healthy fats, it is free from rancid or foreign matter. Suitable for human consumption, with no additives or animal fats. Corn oil supports heart health and offers consistent frying performance. Delivered in custom packaging from 1L to flexitanks as per requirement.

Specifications
  • FFA: Max 0.1%
  • S.G. at 25°C: 0.915–0.922
  • Saponification: 187–193
  • Iodine Number: 103–128
  • Impurities: 0%
  • Color: Light Yellow

Refined Soybean Oil

Made from Glycine max seeds, it’s cholesterol-free and rich in Vitamin E. Suitable for both cooking and salad dressing due to its light texture. It has low saturated fat, ideal for health-conscious consumers. The oil is refined using advanced technology ensuring purity and clarity. Soybean oil has excellent frying stability and retains freshness longer. It is available in a variety of packaging formats to suit global demands.

Specifications
  • FFA: Max 0.3%
  • Refractive Index: 1.466–1.470
  • Iodine Value: 120–143
  • Density: 0.909–0.925
  • Appearance: Clear
  • Saponification: 189–195

RBD Palm Olein CP8

RBD Palm Olein CP8 is a high-quality palm cooking oil known for its clarity and golden color. It remains liquid at room temperature, making it suitable for deep frying and cooking. CP8 grade has a lower cloud point, ideal for cooler climates. It contains natural antioxidants and Vitamin E for improved shelf life. Widely used in the food industry, it enhances flavor and texture of foods. It meets international standards for safe and healthy cooking oil.

Specifications
  • Cloud Point: 8°C
  • Color: Light Golden
  • Free Fatty Acid: Max 0.1%
  • Usage: Frying & Cooking
  • Origin: Malaysia/Indonesia
  • Packing: 1L, 5L, 15L, Bulk

Crude Sunflower Oil

Crude Sunflower Oil is extracted from sunflower seeds and is rich in natural nutrients. It is unrefined, maintaining the original flavor and antioxidants like tocopherols. This oil is widely used in food processing and as a raw material for refining. It has a high linoleic acid content and light amber color. Ideal for blending, refining, or industrial cooking applications. It is supplied in bulk packaging and exported globally.

Specifications
  • FFA: Max 1%
  • Moisture: Max 0.2%
  • Color: Light Amber
  • Purity: 100% Natural
  • Origin: Ukraine/Russia
  • Packing: Bulk Drums or Tanker

Crude Palm Oil

Crude Palm Oil (CPO) is a reddish oil extracted from palm fruit, used in refining and biofuels. It is rich in carotenoids, tocotrienols, and natural antioxidants. This oil is semi-solid at room temperature and has a distinct color and aroma. CPO is used in food, cosmetics, and energy industries. It undergoes refining to produce edible palm olein and stearin. Exported in bulk, it's a key global commodity from Southeast Asia.

Specifications
  • FFA: Max 5%
  • Moisture: Max 0.25%
  • Color: Reddish Orange
  • Usage: Industrial & Refining
  • Origin: Malaysia/Indonesia
  • Packing: Flexi Bags / Bulk Tanker
