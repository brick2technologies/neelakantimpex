About Us

I’m Mahesh Valsa,founder of NEELAKANT –IMPEX India, located in South of India. With over 40 years of experience in rice farming and milling, our journey began by supplying high-quality rice to global markets through trusted traders. Today, we’ve established our own export company to deliver the same premium quality directly to international buyers — at more competitive prices. Our strength lies in sourcing rice straight from our own farms, processing it in our mills, and then exporting it worldwide. This direct farm-to-export model ensures excellent quality control and significantly reduces costs — a benefit we proudly pass on to our clients. We specialize in a wide range of Non-Basmati and Specialty Rice varieties such as 1121 Sella, 1509, Sona Masuri, Swarna Masuri, Ponni, Chella Ponni, IR64, IR36, Brown Rice, Parboiled, Steam, Raw, Sella, and 100% Broken Rice. Every grain we deliver is carefully processed to meet international standards, maintaining its natural taste and nutritional value. Apart from rice, we also supply other agro commodities including Millets, Soy, Sugar, and a variety of vegetable oils like Palm Oil, Sunflower Oil, and Corn Oil. Our commitment is to provide top-quality products with transparent pricing, reliable service, and timely global delivery. At Neelakant – Valsa Exports, you will truly experience the difference in quality and value.
