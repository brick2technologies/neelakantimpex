Agro Products

Our premium selection of agro-based commodities for domestic and international markets.

Wheat

Wheat is one of the world's most important staple crops, rich in carbohydrates and dietary fiber.
Our export-grade wheat is carefully sourced and sorted for superior quality.
Commonly used for bread, pasta, and flour production.
We offer both hard and soft varieties of wheat.
Packed under hygienic conditions in bulk or customized formats.
Sourced from top wheat-producing states in India.

Barley

Barley is a nutrient-rich grain widely used for food, feed, and brewing.
High in dietary fiber, B vitamins, and essential minerals.
We provide hulled and pearl barley options.
Sourced from certified farms ensuring purity and quality.
Available in various packing options as per customer requirements.
Used in soups, health foods, and animal feed industries.

Sorghum (Jowar)

Sorghum, also known as Jowar, is a drought-resistant cereal crop.
Ideal for gluten-free diets and animal fodder.
Packed with proteins, fiber, and antioxidants.
Export quality grains from trusted Indian farms.
Free from harmful chemicals and impurities.
Supplied in clean, food-grade packaging options.

Millets

Millets are traditional grains known for high nutritional value.
Includes varieties like Pearl Millet, Finger Millet, Foxtail Millet.
Rich in iron, calcium, and amino acids.
Popular in health-conscious diets worldwide.
We offer cleaned and polished millets ready for export.
Excellent shelf life and secure packaging.

Pulses

Pulses like lentils, chickpeas, and mung beans are essential protein sources.
High-quality pulses for cooking and export available in bulk.
Cleaned, sorted, and graded for consistency.
Custom packaging and labeling options available.
Sourced from major pulse-producing states in India.
Rich in protein, fiber, and essential nutrients.

Oilseeds

We offer a wide range of oilseeds including mustard, sesame, and groundnut.
Sourced from well-managed farms for premium quality.
Suitable for extraction of cooking oils and industrial uses.
Cleaned, graded, and packed for global export.
Rich in essential fatty acids and antioxidants.
Delivered in jute bags, PP bags, or custom packaging.
